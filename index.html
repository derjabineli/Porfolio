About Me

Hey there! My name is Eli Derjabin. I am an aspiring Web Developer. I never expected myself to pursue a career withing Developing. However, after spending some time studying Business Marketing I quickly realized that marketing was not something that I wanted to do with the rest of my life. After some time of being uncertain of what I wanted to do, I decided to attempt some online modules that taught the basics of C++. My desire to further understand developing grew from there. After a few months of working on these online modules I decided to change my major and begin pursuing Computer Science as my career.
